*** About ***
This demo runs on STM32 Nucleo-64 F401RE. The aim of this demo is to proof
the usage of the nRF24L01 library.

*** Related article ***
For more information read the article "A Radio Frequency transceiver library:
nRF24L01 and ChibiOS/RT".
http://www.playembedded.org/blog/a-radio-frequency-transceiver-library-nrf24l01-and-chibiosrt/

*** ChibiOS related version ***
Tested under ChibiOS 19.1.0
